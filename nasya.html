Nasya (Nasal Therapy)

 
 
   
     
       

Duration: 5–7 days (sometimes 14).

       

What it involves:

       
             
  • Medicated oil drops instilled daily, usually after a short massage & steam of face/neck.
    •          
  • Takes ~30 minutes per session.
    •        
     
         
   
     
       

Benefits & Relevance:

       

Effective for sinusitis, migraines, headaches, cervical spondylosis, and memory enhancement. Widely included in wellness resorts and occupational health programs.

       

Main Sector of Influence:

       

Head, neck, and sensory organs — especially respiratory passages and nervous system support.
