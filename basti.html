Basti (Medicated Enema)

Duration: 5–8 days (up to 30 for chronic issues).

What it involves:

  • Done daily or on alternate days.
  • Two types: Niruha (decoction) & Anuvasana (oil) often in planned sequence.

Benefits & Relevance:

Basti is considered the prime therapy for balancing Vata dosha. It helps in arthritis, back pain, neurological conditions, and chronic constipation. Extensively used in Ayurveda clinics and rehab centers.

Main Sector of Influence:

Musculoskeletal and nervous system health, pain management, and mobility restoration.
