What is Panchakarma?

Panchakarma is the cornerstone of Ayurveda’s detoxification and rejuvenation therapies. It involves five primary procedures (Vamana, Virechana, Basti, Nasya, and Raktamokshana) that eliminate accumulated toxins, restore balance, and promote holistic well-being.

These therapies are relevant for preventive healthcare, chronic disease management, and wellness programs in hospitals, spas, and wellness resorts.
