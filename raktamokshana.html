Raktamokshana (Bloodletting)

 
 
   
     
       

Duration: 1–3 sessions (spaced over days/weeks).

       

What it involves:

       
             
  • Done only if indicated.
    •          
  • Leeches or controlled puncture used on specific days.
    •          
  • Local aftercare for 1–2 days.
    •        
     
         
   
     
       

Benefits & Relevance:

       

Useful for certain skin diseases, localized inflammation, and detoxification of blood. Practiced under strict clinical conditions in Ayurveda hospitals.

       

Main Sector of Influence:

       

Circulatory and dermatological health, localized toxin removal.
