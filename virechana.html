Virechana (Therapeutic Purgation)

Duration: 1 main day of purgation + 3–5 days prep + 2–3 days recovery.

What it involves:

  • Similar preparation as Vamana.
  • Purgation medicine given once in the morning.
  • Rest & gradual diet for 2–3 days after.

Benefits & Relevance:

Beneficial for liver disorders, hyperacidity, skin diseases, digestive imbalance, and excess Pitta dosha. Used in therapeutic detox programs, hospitals, and corporate wellness packages.

Main Sector of Influence:

Digestive and hepatic health, detoxification of the GI tract.
