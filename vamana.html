Vamana (Therapeutic Emesis)

Duration: 1 main day of emesis + 3–5 days preparation + 2–3 days recovery.

What it involves:

  • 3–5 days of Snehana (ghee/oil intake) & Swedana (steam) to loosen toxins.
  • Emesis done early morning on one specific day.
  • Light diet & rest for 2–3 days after.

Benefits & Relevance:

Vamana is especially effective for respiratory conditions, skin disorders, obesity, and excess Kapha dosha. It’s widely used in Ayurveda hospitals, wellness centers, and preventive healthcare programs.

Main Sector of Influence:

Respiratory and metabolic health, allergy management, and chronic sinusitis care.
